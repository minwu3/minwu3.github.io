Dec 2021

House Price Prediction in Python

In this project, we developed and evaluated the performance and the predictive power of a model trained and tested on data collected from houses in King County, USA. Once we get a good fit, we will use this model to predict the monetary value of a house located at the King County.

The ultimate goal of the project is to build a prediction engine capable of predicting districts median housing price. We know that this is supervised learning problem as our data set consists of labelled observations and it does looks like multivariate regression should be our got to option but we will explore multiple ways of building the model and finally pick the one with lowest error rate RMSE (Root Mean Square Error) or MAE (Mean Absolute Error).
